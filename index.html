What We Do

Japan-side context, translated into practical recruitment decisions.

Japan Talent Desk helps European clubs filter the Japanese market into practical next-step recruitment decisions. The work is intentionally narrow: role fit, deal realism, contract and availability questions, local-source context, and whether a profile is ready for further review.

  • Club-specific recruitment notes
  • Role-specific shortlist support
  • Contract and availability questions
  • Local-source context and Europe-readiness
